Upper & Lower Eyelid Lift with Fat Transfer

Surgical

Combined Blepharoplasty and Fat Transfer

This procedure addresses both upper and lower eyelids: excess skin and fat are removed or repositioned, and your own fat is transferred to smooth hollows and restore volume under the eyes. The result is a refreshed, natural look with improved contour and minimal signs of surgery.

Dr. Nandan carefully plans incision placement and fat harvest to achieve balance and longevity. Your consultation will cover whether this combined approach is right for you and what to expect during recovery.
