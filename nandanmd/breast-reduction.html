Breast Reduction

Surgical

Reduction Mammaplasty

Breast reduction removes excess breast tissue, fat, and skin to achieve a smaller, lighter, and more proportional breast size. Many patients experience relief from back, neck, and shoulder pain, as well as improved comfort during exercise and daily activities.

Dr. Nandan uses precise techniques to reshape and reposition the breast and nipple-areola complex for a natural, balanced result. Your consultation will include a discussion of incision options, recovery, and whether insurance may apply.
