Eyelid Surgery

Surgical

Blepharoplasty

Eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) addresses drooping upper lids, puffiness, and excess skin or fat around the eyes to create a more rested, refreshed appearance. It can also improve vision when heavy lids obstruct the visual field.

Dr. Nandan offers upper eyelid lift, lower eyelid surgery, and combined procedures — including upper and lower eyelid lift with fat transfer for a smooth, natural result. See the dedicated pages below for more detail.
