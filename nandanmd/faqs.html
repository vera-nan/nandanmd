Common Questions

What should I expect at my first consultation?
Your consultation is a private, thorough discussion of your goals and options. We will review your medical history, examine the area of concern, and recommend a personalized plan. You can ask any questions about the procedure, recovery, and expected outcomes.
Do you accept insurance?
We accept all major insurances. Coverage depends on the procedure; some aesthetic procedures may be out-of-pocket. We can help clarify benefits and payment options.
How long is recovery typically?
Recovery varies by procedure. We will give you a clear timeline and aftercare instructions at your consultation and follow up with you through each stage.
Where are procedures performed?
Procedures are performed in accredited facilities. Details are discussed during your consultation.
Is financing or payment plans available?
We accept all major insurances where applicable. For cosmetic procedures, we can discuss payment options and any financing available through our practice or partners at your consultation.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Please bring a photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical history or prior surgical records. A list of questions or goals is helpful. We will send any intake forms in advance when possible.
