Tummy Tuck

Surgical

Abdominoplasty

A tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) removes excess skin and fat from the abdomen and tightens weakened or separated muscles to create a flatter, firmer contour. It is often chosen by patients after pregnancy or significant weight loss when diet and exercise cannot address loose skin or muscle laxity.

Dr. Nandan tailors the procedure — full, mini, or extended — to your anatomy and goals. Liposuction may be combined for optimal contour. Your consultation will cover incision placement, recovery, and long-term care.
