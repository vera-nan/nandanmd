Breast Lift

Surgical

Mastopexy

A breast lift (mastopexy) raises and reshapes sagging breasts by removing excess skin and tightening surrounding tissue. It can restore a more youthful contour and position of the nipple and areola. Dr. Nandan tailors the technique to your anatomy and goals for a natural, balanced result.

Ideal for women who have experienced changes due to pregnancy, weight loss, or aging. If you also desire more volume, augmentation can be combined with a lift — we will discuss your options at your consultation.
