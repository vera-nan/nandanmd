Your Surgeon

Raghu Nandan, MD, MPH, FACS

Dr. Nandan is an aesthetic and reconstructive plastic surgeon practicing in the Bay Area. He specializes in aesthetic surgery of the face, breast, and body, as well as complex head-to-toe reconstructive surgery. He completed his plastic surgery training at Johns Hopkins Hospital, ranked #1 by U.S. News & World Report for 23 consecutive years, and is double board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Surgery.
