# Nayak Plastic Surgery — What to Adopt for Nandan MD

Review of [Nayak Plastic Surgery](https://www.nayakplasticsurgery.com/) and recommendations for nandanmd.com.

---

## What Makes Nayak’s Site Strong

### 1. **Hero + social proof above the fold**
- **Nayak:** “St. Louis Plastic Surgery” headline; badges: Instagram 475K, RealSelf Hall of Fame, Castle Connolly Top Doctors, St. Louis Magazine.
- **Nayak:** Tagline: “Where Excellence speaks for itself” / “Respected by the experts. Chosen by the world.”
- **For Nandan:** Add a short **emotional tagline** under your hero (e.g. “Respected by experts. Chosen by discerning patients.”). Keep or subtly expand the trust bar with “Rated by patients on RealSelf & Doximity” so review proof is visible without scrolling.

### 2. **Transformation story**
- **Nayak:** “Your beauty story is one of transformation… You can entrust Dr. Nayak to craft the perfect plastic surgery plan.”
- **Nandan:** You already have “Refinement. Precision. Discretion.” and “Personalized care for discerning patients.” Consider one line that explicitly uses **“transformation”** or **“entrust”** (e.g. “Entrust your transformation to a surgeon who combines artistry with rigor.”) in the About or Why Choose section.

### 3. **“One destination”**
- **Nayak:** “One Destination for total aesthetic care” with sections: Laser & Energy, Skin Rejuvenation, Face, Breast & Body, Injectables & Fillers — each with procedure lists.
- **Nandan:** Add a **“One destination”** block on the home page: “One destination for face, eyelids, breast & body” with quick links to Surgical Procedures, Non-Surgical, and Photo Gallery so visitors see the full scope in one place.

### 4. **Patient quotes on the home page**
- **Nayak:** Rotating quotes (“I look like ME, but like I looked a decade or so ago,” “Dr Nayak and his staff exceeded my expectations”) and “Our Patients Say it Best” with full testimonials + “5 Stars from 625+ Reviews.”
- **Nandan:** Add a **“What patients say”** strip on the home page: 2–3 short pull quotes and a link to Patient Testimonials. If you have a star count (Google, RealSelf), add “X Stars from Y+ Reviews” in the strip or footer.

### 5. **Meet the surgeon + credentials**
- **Nayak:** “Meet Dr. Nayak” / “Meet Dr. Kachare” with video, credentials (Yale, Harvard, Washington U, board certs), and specialty procedures. “Triple Board-Certified,” “Clinical Professor of the Year.”
- **Nandan:** You have a strong About block and “Why Choose.” Optional: a dedicated **Meet Dr. Nandan** page with a short video (if you add one), selected publications, and 1–2 awards if applicable.

### 6. **Media / recognition**
- **Nayak:** “The Authority in Surgical Excellence” with logos (People, NYT, Newsweek, Allure, Castle Connolly, etc.) and copy: “earned acclaim in top publications… Newsweek named him one of America’s Best Plastic Surgeons.”
- **Nandan:** If Dr. Nandan has been featured (press, Top Doctor lists, teaching awards), add an **“As seen in”** or **“Recognition”** strip with logos or names and one sentence. If not yet, skip until you have clips.

### 7. **Why choose / what makes us different**
- **Nayak:** “Why Choose Nayak Plastic Surgery?” + education focus (videos, demystifying the process).
- **Nandan:** You already have “Why choose Dr. Nandan.” Optional: add a line about **patient education** (e.g. “We take time to explain your options so you can make informed decisions”) to mirror Nayak’s education angle.

### 8. **Team**
- **Nayak:** “The Team for your dream” — photos and roles (surgical assistant, nurses, coordinators).
- **Nandan:** If you have a small team (coordinator, nurse), a **“Our team”** section with names and roles builds trust. Optional.

### 9. **Procedure cards on home**
- **Nayak:** Grid of procedure cards (Breast Augmentation, Breast Lift, Mommy Makeover, etc.) with image, title, short blurb, and link.
- **Nandan:** You have procedure pages and a Photo Gallery grid. Optional: a **“Popular procedures”** strip on the home page (e.g. Breast Lift, Tummy Tuck, Eyelid Surgery) with one line each and link to the procedure page.

### 10. **Videos**
- **Nayak:** “Watch Dr. Nayak’s Video” / “Watch Dr. Kachare Video,” plus Educational Videos, Post-Op Videos, Video Testimonials.
- **Nandan:** If you have a **welcome or “Meet Dr. Nandan” video**, add a section with thumbnail and play button. If not, add a placeholder line: “Ask about our consultation process and what to expect” and add video later.

### 11. **Resources**
- **Nayak:** Financing, Specials, Policies & Paperwork, FAQ, Out-of-Town Patients.
- **Nandan:** You have FAQs (including financing). Optional: **“Consultation process”** or **“What to expect”** page; **“Out-of-town patients”** only if you target them (e.g. Bay Area visitors).

### 12. **Concierge / out-of-town**
- **Nayak:** “Concierge plastic surgery services” — virtual consults, travel accommodations, coordinators.
- **Nandan:** Add only if you offer **travel coordination or virtual consultations** for out-of-area patients; otherwise skip.

### 13. **Footer**
- **Nayak:** Multiple locations with hours, phone, “5.0 from 1059+ Reviews,” email signup, Accessibility statement + accessibility menu (contrast, font size, etc.).
- **Nandan:** Add **location + hours** (e.g. “Campbell, CA — Contact for hours” or real hours). You already have review line and accessibility note. Optional: **email signup** (“Stay in touch”) if you have a list; **accessibility menu** (e.g. contrast, larger text) if you want to match Nayak’s compliance level.

### 14. **Email capture**
- **Nayak:** “Join Our VIP Email List! Be the first to hear about procedures, specials, updates.”
- **Nandan:** Optional **footer email signup** (e.g. “Stay in touch — new procedures and practice updates”) wired to your email provider or Formspree.

5. **Footer** — Location line: “Campbell, CA — Contact for hours” (or your real hours when you set them).

Optional next steps: dedicated Meet Dr. Nandan page with video, “As seen in” if you have press, team section, procedure cards on home, email signup, accessibility menu.
